What are your delivery charges?

Free delivery on orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, delivery charges are ₹50 within Mumbai and ₹100 for other cities.

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Contact our sales team for custom quotes on large quantity purchases.

Are all products certified organic?

All our organic products are certified by recognized authorities. Product certifications are clearly mentioned on each product page.

What is your return policy?
